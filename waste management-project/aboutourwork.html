ABOUT OUR WORK

We believe in three key pillars to Solid Waste Management (SWM) – micro-planning, decentralized management (especially of wet waste) and stakeholder engagement.
The “Polluter Pays” principle is a strong driver of the process. While communities or citizens may not always have the time to plan SWM systems for their areas, it is definitely possible to engage them in a way such that they own upto their responsibilities as waste generators.
